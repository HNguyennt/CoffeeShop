ABOUT US

ABOUT LAMBORE COFFEE

With over twenty years in the industry, our team takes pride in providing all of our customers with quality products and service. We strive to create a family-oriented relationship with all of our clients and understand that your satisfaction is the key to our success.

Keeping up with the times and the trends, we make sure that our coffee is always meeting the needs and tastes of every individual. Considering the uniqueness of our each one of our customers, not only do we take pride in working with our clients to develop a coffee program that best suits the needs of their customer base, we also help ensure that our products are a success in their establishment as well. Our company offers a broad variety of coffee from around the world, including specialty coffee. We also do private labeling for companies who wish to have our coffee sold under their business' name.
